## 📝 Notes for Developer

1. **Logo Usage:** Use `Bees_Logo.png` as the primary logo. Use `website_logo_transparent_background.png` when placing logo over colored backgrounds.

2. **Product Categories to Build:**
   - Spices & Masala
   - Ayurveda & Herbal
   - Tea & Coffee
   - Seed Oils & Cooking Oils
   - Marine Products / Seafood
   - Carpets & Rugs
   - Leather Goods
   - Garments & Textiles
   - Handicrafts
   - Rubber Products
   - Raw Materials
   - Agricultural Products
   - Processed Food
   - Fruits & Vegetables

3. **Color Scheme (from original site):**
   - Primary: Green (agriculture/organic feel)
   - Secondary: Orange/Gold (Indian export branding)
   - Accent: Dark Blue/Navy (trust/corporate)
   - Background: White/Light Gray

4. **Typography:** The original used Google Fonts (Poppins/Open Sans family)

5. **Key Pages to Build:**
   - Home (hero banner + product categories + about section)
   - Products/Shop (category grid with filters)
   - Individual Category Pages
   - About Us (company info + APEDA certification)
   - Contact Us (form + map + details)
   - Enquiry/Quote Request
